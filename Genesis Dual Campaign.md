### Common Rules

- **One address, one campaign** – smart contract rejects duplicates and cross‑participation.
- **On‑chain real‑time settlement** – rewards deducted immediately from pool upon claim.

- **First‑come, first‑served** – claims processed until pool depleted.
- **All rewards (including Grand Prize) must be actively claimed** – no automatic transfers.
